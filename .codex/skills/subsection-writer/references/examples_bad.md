# Bad Examples

These are anti-patterns to rewrite, not templates to keep.

## Bad opener

- `This subsection surveys recent approaches in the area.`

Why it fails:
- says what the writer is doing, not what the literature shows
- interchangeable across topics

## Bad contrast

- `Many papers do X, while other papers do Y.`

Why it fails:
- no axis
- no consequence
- no reason the comparison matters

## Bad synthesis filler

- `Additional evidence reinforces the same pattern from a different angle.`

Why it fails:
- generic filler shape
- usually added for length, not coverage
- can be pasted into almost any subsection

## Bad limitation

- `Two limitations stand out.`

Why it fails:
- obvious slot phrase
- encourages repeated cadence across sections
