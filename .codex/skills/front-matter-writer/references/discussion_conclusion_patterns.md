# Discussion / Conclusion Patterns

## Goal

Keep `Discussion` and `Conclusion` comparative, evidence-bounded, and free of workflow narration.

## Discussion should do

- synthesize recurring trade-offs across chapters
- explain why apparently similar systems diverge under different protocols, budgets, or threat models
- identify which evaluation and deployment assumptions most affect interpretation

## Conclusion should do

- restate the comparative lens in compact form
- emphasize what current evidence supports reliably
- point to the most important unresolved comparison gaps without drifting into proposal prose

## Avoid

- pipeline/process narration
- domain-default claims that only fit one narrow topic
- generic closure stems such as "the remainder of the paper" or "this survey provides an overview"
- count-based slot phrasing such as "three takeaways" unless the evidence genuinely supports that structure
