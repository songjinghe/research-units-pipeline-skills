# Good Examples

Paraphrase these patterns; do not copy them verbatim.

## Good opener

- `A central tension is not whether the route improves capability in the abstract, but which assumptions make those gains reproducible once tools, budgets, and failure handling are fixed.`

Why it works:
- content-bearing
- comparison-ready
- no self-narration

## Good contrast sentence

- `Retrieval-heavy designs keep state verifiable at read time, whereas write-heavy memory shifts the burden to logging and contamination control, so the two routes fail differently under the same benchmark protocol.`

Why it works:
- direct A-vs-B contrast
- mechanism tied to consequence
- evaluation relevance is visible

## Good limitation sentence

- `That comparison remains fragile when retry policy and tool permissions are reported unevenly, because those settings can change both success rate and cost without changing the nominal architecture.`

Why it works:
- concrete caveat
- changes interpretation
- no slot phrasing
