## Failures / limitations

- Indoor mobile manipulation (MoMA) enables robots to translate natural language instructions into physical actions, yet long-horizon execution remains challenging due to cascading errors and limited generalization across diverse environments. Li2026Momastage
- Learning-based approaches often fail to maintain logical consistency over extended horizons, while methods relying on explicit scene representations impose rigid structural assumptions that reduce adaptability in dynamic settings. Li2026Momastage
- Recent advances in vision, language, and multimodal learning have substantially accelerated progress in robotic foundation models, with robot manipulation remaining a central and challenging problem. Bai2025Embodied
- Finally: we identify open challenges and prospective research directions related to scalability, data efficiency, multimodal physical interaction, and safety. Bai2025Embodied
- However is a significant gap exists between benchmarks designed for high-level language instruction following, which often assume perfect low-level execution, and those for low-level robot control, which rely on simple, one-step commands. Kachaev2025Mind
- This disconnect prevents a comprehensive evaluation of integrated systems where both task planning and physical execution are critical. Kachaev2025Mind
- Kitchen-R bridges a key gap in embodied AI research, enabling more holistic and realistic benchmarking of language-guided robotic agents. Kachaev2025Mind
- These requirements increase system cost and integration complexity, thus hindering practical deployment for lightweight UAVs. Xu2025Aerial
