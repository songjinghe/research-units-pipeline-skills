# Survey Visuals — Timeline Patterns

## Milestone Selection Rules

1. **High-signal over high-volume**: aim for 8–12 milestones, not one per paper
2. **Paradigm shifts**: prioritize papers that changed how the field works (new loop, new evaluation, new scale)
3. **Priority papers first**: prefer papers marked `priority: high` in notes
4. **Year spread**: ensure coverage across the study period, not just recent papers
5. **Citation density**: if a paper appears in many subsection mappings, it's a stronger milestone candidate

## Timeline Entry Format

```
- YEAR: TITLE (`paper_id`) [@bibkey]
```

- Each entry must have at least one `[@...]` citation marker
- Keep the description implicit in the title; do not add narrative prose
- If a year has multiple milestones, group them but keep total under 12

## Anti-Patterns

- ❌ One bullet per paper in the core set (that's a bibliography, not a timeline)
- ❌ Milestones without citation markers
- ❌ Narrative commentary like "This was a breakthrough" (let the reader judge from context)
