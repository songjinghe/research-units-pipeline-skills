- A second challenge is the hallucination from domain shift. Chen2025Owmm

- Hydrodynamic uncertainty, partial observability, bandwidth-limited communication, and energy scarcity are not independent challenges; they interact within the closed perception-planning-control loop and often amplify one another over time. Xu2026Underwater
- However: modeling these world dynamics, especially for dexterous robotics tasks, poses significant challenges due to limited data coverage and scarce action labels. Gao2026Dreamdojo
- Systematic evaluation on multiple challenging out-of-distribution (OOD) benchmarks verifies the significance of our method for simulating open-world, contact-rich tasks, paving the way for general-purpose robot world models. Gao2026Dreamdojo
