# Embodied AI Domain Pack

Use this pack when the survey is about embodied AI, robot foundation models, VLA systems, embodied manipulation, navigation, or physically grounded agents.

The pack is designed to keep the taxonomy paper-like:

- task and embodiment scope first
- architectures and policy/world-model choices second
- data/training/post-training third
- evaluation/safety/deployment last

It deliberately avoids collapsing the whole topic into a single "VLA methods" bucket.

The intended comparison lenses are:

- embodiment and interface assumptions
- policy/action representation
- planning and world-model use
- data/supervision and post-training
- evaluation realism, safety, and deployment readiness
