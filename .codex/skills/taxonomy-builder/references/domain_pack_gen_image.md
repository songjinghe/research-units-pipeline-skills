# Domain Pack: Generative Image Models

This pack targets **text-to-image / generative image model** corpora where diffusion, token-based generation, editing, conditioning, and evaluation/safety are central organizing questions.

## Scope cues

In scope:
- diffusion / latent diffusion / DiT-style backbones
- text-to-image generation and editing
- conditioning / control / personalization
- evaluation, robustness, provenance, and safety for generative image models

Usually out of scope unless central to the corpus:
- general computer vision papers with no generation task
- image-only downstream applications that do not reorganize the model taxonomy
- generic multimodal papers where image generation is incidental rather than central

## Why these four top-level chapters

- **Foundations & Formulations**: objectives, representations, and sampling/inference assumptions
- **Model Families (Diffusion & Token-based)**: backbone families and their practical trade-offs
- **Control, Editing & Conditioning**: how generation is steered or adapted
- **Evaluation, Safety & Provenance**: how model quality, robustness, and misuse are assessed

This pack is intentionally compact so the downstream outline can stay paper-like rather than exploding into many thin chapters.
